    Research Unit 6

    RU6-WEB
    (2014)

    The RU6's website. Research Unit 6 (RU6) is part of the R&D Department of CTI & Press "Diophantus". RU6 exhibits research activity in Mobile Networks, Next Generation Networks, Cross-layer Design and Mechanisms, Network Simulations and other areas.

    As a member of RU6 I was assigned the task of creating the new RU6's website and migrate all the content from its previous one.

    I built the site with Drupal. Used many community modules (Views, multilingual, biblio, contextual filters, feeds, rules, custom modules, drush, foothemes ...) and added a few custom ones.

    (2013)

    Events handler and automatisation of devices with Uberdust system. Minutes before an event, the lights and air conditioning start automatically. The interface is implemented as a Drupal module. Me and 2 colleagues implemented this interface.
