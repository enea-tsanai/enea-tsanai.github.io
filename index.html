Hello!

I am an international Software Engineering graduate student at Villanova University. I started on August 2015 and I am expected to graduate on May 2017.

I was born in Vlore, Albania but I lived in Greece my whole life. I studied for Computer Engineering and Informatics in University of Patras, Greece and I earned my diploma on November 2014.

I enjoy problem solving and translating solutions into code. As an undergraduate student I got exposed to a plethora of team projects and challenges where I had the chance to learn more and more. I got involved in research and development and published several papers. My fields of interest include desktop/web/mobile applications, databases and game development.

I am a fan of drawing on paper, listening to music, swimming and dirt bikes! However, many times I end up watching development tutorials or playing around with a previous project!
